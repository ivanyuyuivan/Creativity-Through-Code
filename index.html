Clash of Clans Troops: Barbarians

The Barbarian is a kilt-clad Scottish warrior with an angry, battle-ready expression, hungry for destruction. He has close-cropped blonde hair and a long yellow horseshoe mustache. Barbarians are melee ground troops. They will attack any opponent's troops, skeletons, and heroes BATTLING with them, and when the other troops are dead, they will move on the the closest building to them. The Barbarian is unlocked with Barracks level 1. The Barbarian takes up one space in your army camps.

Clash of Clans Troops: Archers

The Archer is a female warrior with sharp eyes. She wears a short, light green dress, a hooded cape, a leather belt and an attached small pouch. She has a quiver slung to her back, a shortbow in her left hand and a golden band on each of her upper arms. She is the first ranged unit you unlock. They will attack any opponent's troops, skeletons, or heroes BATTLING with them, and when the other troops are dead, they will move on the the closest building to them. The Archer is unlocked with Barracks level 2. The Archer takes up one space in your army camps.
